FAQ
Is Quiz What? for free?
Quiz What? can be downloaded for free and Quiz What? Pro is the premium version.
What is the difference between the free version of Quiz What? and the premium version Quiz What? Pro?
Quiz What? Pro and Quiz What? mainly differ in terms of the amount of quizzes. In addition to this, Quiz What? Pro does not show any ads and the amount of QUIZZITs to reveal hints is unlimited. Moreover, the premium version accompanies your quiz journey with some sound effects and more ranks and entertaining success messages are waiting for you. Due to the unlimited QUIZZITs the calculation of the points is also handled differently in the two app versions.
Is there an iOS Version of the app?
No, currently Quiz What? and Quiz What? Pro are available on the Google Play platform, only. Because of limited resources, the distribution of the app was narrowed down to Android devices.
Is an iOS version planned?
At the moment an iOS version is not planned.
What quiz categories are available in Quiz What?
Public figures (movie stars, singers, politicians, artists, scientists,etc.), movies (from various genres), games and brands!
How many quizzes can I solve in the free version?
Around 140 quizzes will keep you entertained, consisting of 50 public figures, 50 movies, 20 games and 20 brands quizzes. What are you waiting for - download and enjoy ;)
How many quizzes are provided in the premium version Quiz What? Pro?
Are you ready for solving more than 1200 quizzes? If yes, 450 public figures, 450 movies, more than 100 games and more than 200 brands quizzes will challenge your quiz skills.
What type of hints can I use in Quiz What? and Quiz What? Pro?
The main goal of the app is to guess the solution with the help of emojis. Thus, you can add up to 9 smiley hints to figure out what or who we are looking for. Should the smiley hints not be enough, feel free to use character hints - revealing one character at a time. If that does not help either, there is also the superhint with which the solution should instantly come to your mind. The superhint will present to you a phrase with a clear indication about the quiz. How many hints will you need? Remember: the less, the better!
How can I reveal a smiley hint?
Simply use the ADD SMILEY button.
How can I reveal a character hint?
For that, just use the ADD HINT button.
How can I reveal the superhint?
The superhint is revealed by the light bulb icon.
What are the QUIZZITs and what do I need them for?
QUIZZITs are the credits you will need in the Quiz What? free version to reveal character hints and the superhints. When you have downloaded the app, you will be provided with 10 QUIZZIT points. Use them wisely, since you have to refill them, once you have used all QUIZZITs. You can gain more QUIZZITs by watching ads. Or you switch to the premium version Quiz What? Pro and there you have an unlimited resource of QUIZZITs.
How many QUIZZITs are used for the hints?
A character hint requires one QUIZZIT, whereas a superhint will need five QUIZZITs.
What is the Mixed Quiz Mode?
You can either solve quizzes in one of the four quiz categories (Public figures, movies, games or brands) or you choose the mixed mode where a quiz is taken from any of the categories and presented to you. Do you strive for greater challenges? Then go for this mode!
How many points can I achieve for one quiz?
In Quiz What? and Quiz What? Pro 1000 points can be achieved per quiz.
How is the score calculated in Quiz What? and Quiz What? Pro?
The free version only considers the number of revealed smileys in the calculation of the score. For that, the total number of achievable points (1000 points) is divided by the total number of smileys that can be revealed for the quiz. This fraction is multiplied with the number of revealed smileys when the quiz is solved and the result is subtracted from the total points.
Example: Movie XY is described with 6 smileys (i.e. 5 revealable smileys, since the first one is always shown). Each revealable smiley is worth 200 points (1000 divided by 5). Consequently, if you reveal 2 smileys, you will lose 400 points (2 x 200 points). Hence, the final score for this quiz will amount to 600 points (1000 - 400 points).

The premium version also takes revealed characters and superhint into consideration. The approach is quite similar to the free version, however the fraction is calculated slightly differently. The total points are not divided only by the total amount of revealable smileys, but by the total number of revealable smileys AND characters. When the quiz is solved, the fraction is multiplied by the sum of the revealed smileys AND characters and subtracted from the total score of 1000 points. If a superhint was used, the result is divided by 2
Example: Movie XY consists of five characters and is described with 6 smileys (i.e. 5 revealable smileys, since the first one is always shown). Each revealable smiley OR character is worth 100 points (1000 points divided by (5 revealable smileys + 5 revealable characters) = 100 points). Assume you solved the quiz by revealing 3 smileys and 4 characters and the use of the superhint. You will lose 700 points based on the used smiley and character hints ((3 + 4) x 100 = 700 points). 1000 - 700 = 300 points. Since you used the superhint the result is divided by 2. 300 / 2 = 150. So your score will total 150 points for this quiz.
Any tips how to solve the quizzes?
In general, it is up to you how you want to solve the quiz - using more character or smiley hints. Regarding the interpretation of the smileys, use your imagination and try different approaches in guessing the result. This is because even the same smiley can be used as a different type of clue in different quizzes.
Example: a flag smiley can be used to indicate the place of birth of a public figure in one quiz. In another quiz, the same flag indicates an important destination in the plot of a movie. In a third quiz, the name of the country represented by the flag might be the title of a song performed by a singer who is the solution of the quiz. Sometimes multiple revealed smileys have to be interpreted together.
Example: The quiz solution is an actress who has played in a movie called e.g. "Tiger City". In the quiz there will be two smileys, a tiger smiley and a city skyline smiley, which together represent this movie title. Those smileys are always placed in sequence, though displayed one at a time every time you click on the ADD SMILEY button. They are never interrupted by a smiley that represents a completely different clue (e.g. a trophy smiley which indicates that the actress has won an Oscar). Long story short, try to use multiple ways of guessing the result and interpret the various hints together! Apart from that, have fun solving the quizzes!
